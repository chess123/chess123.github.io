WELCOME TO MY WEBSITE!!!!

I'm Kesav Viswanadha, a 16-year-old student at Monta Vista High School in Cupertino, CA. I am an International Master (IM) of chess - I travel all over the world to play in tournaments, and I really enjoy it! I also sing Carnatic music and have performed several times in the Bay Area. In my free time, I enjoy programming, playing video games, and basketball. Here are some achievements I'm most proud of:
  • North American Junior (U20) Chess Championship Gold Medalist 2015 - earned me my first Grandmaster norm!
  • North American Youth (U16) Chess Championship Gold Medalist 2014 - earned me the FM title and my first IM norm!
  • 5-time member of the prestigious All-America Chess Team (2012 - 2016)
  • Bronze and Silver medals on the National French Contest in 2014 and 2015 respectively
  • Volunteer tutor at Reading Partners, an organization that helps students behind their grade level in reading realize their full potential, since October 2015.
